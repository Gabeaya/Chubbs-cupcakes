The Legend of Chubbs

Although the story of Chubbs is epic, his orgins are humble. Chubbs simmons was a young orphan who ran away at the age of six. After running with wolves, stealing honey from bears, and climbing through vast wheat mountains he found refuge in a forrest with a widowed librarian. The librarian was a heartbroken man hellbent on his search for knowledge. Therefore he had rare collection of books. Upon the trials and tribulations the two overcame, there was a silver lining to their toubles. Chubbs had a big heart and even a bigger stomach. Reaching to find a solution to the heartbroken caretaker, He used his passion and an old script written by Amelia Simmons herself to create a treat that could mend any sorrow: The Chubbs Cupcake. The Librarian and Chubbs decided to use this to fix the lost souls of the world and now they can fix yours too. But hurry!
